Welcome to Sergio's library!

Here you will find reviews and opinions for movies, actors and directors. This page is divided in three sections: Movies, Actors and Directors. In each one you can filter by Release date, or by Genre and Alphabetical order. The point is that this library is organizad so you can navigate through easily.

  • Objetive
    • Give a rigorous and detailed reviews in form of essays about 600 to 1000 words. Not just like a review briefing
    • Being pure objetive
    • A quantiative view of rating movies and acting. Giving scores to some aspects in the movie, basicaly the ones who are considered in the Oscars such as cinematography, music, screenplay ,etc.
    • To rate actors is kind of different it is measured in a specific way it is possible to capture and for instance give a precise score to the acting. A lot comes from the idea of being more strict if the actor has more time on screen.
  • Vision
    • This is not going to be a page like rotten tomatoes or imdb
    • This page will last until i get tired of watching movies and tired of rating
    • I want to fill the database with more movies and reviews
    • I will want the opinion of other people on my reviews and ratings
  • Important Considerations
    • Every review at the end of the day is just my opinion but i will try to empathize with every perspective so i can give a more precised review without lacking of details and not abusing of generality.
    • The scores for acting and movies also will come from my opinion. But at least for acting there will be some calculus behind to give a precise score. Obviously i will accept discuss of the calculus behind if some don't agree.
    • Every picture or score is taken from another websites for instance the link or reference to those sources are included
